Meeting recorder

Ready to record

Audio stays on your device — never uploaded

How to use

  1. Join Teams or Zoom in another browser tab
  2. Click Start recording below
  3. In Chrome's share dialog, select that tab
  4. Confirm Share tab audio is checked, then click Share

Using the desktop app? Choose Window or Entire screen and enable system audio instead.

Format: .webm  ·  plays in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, VLC
To convert to MP3: open VLC → Media → Convert/Save

Open in Chrome or Edge  ·  Save to desktop or bookmark for quick access
Icons load from CDN (requires internet)  ·  all recording stays on your machine
