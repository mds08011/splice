Desktop — both sides of the call
  1. Join Teams/Zoom (or Phone Link) as usual
  2. Click Start Recording, allow the microphone
  3. In the share dialog, pick the meeting tab or entire screen
  4. Enable Share tab/system audio, then Share

Mic + system audio are mixed so your voice and remote parties are both recorded.

Field / mobile — microphone
  1. Allow microphone access when prompted
  2. Keep this screen open (wake lock is requested)
  3. Place the device centrally for in-person meetings
  4. Stop when finished, then review transcript
