Desktop — both sides of the call
  1. Join Teams/Zoom (or Phone Link) as usual
  2. Click Start Recording, allow the microphone
  3. In the share dialog, pick the meeting tab or entire screen
  4. Enable Share tab/system audio, then Share

Mic + system audio are mixed so your voice and remote parties are both recorded. Long meetings (30–90+ min) are auto-optimized and chunked for final transcription; Download audio stays full quality.

Field / mobile — microphone
  1. Allow microphone access when prompted
  2. Keep this screen open (wake lock is requested)
  3. Place the device centrally for in-person meetings
  4. Stop when finished, then review transcript

Long field sessions are supported: the final pass optimizes and splits audio as needed. Your download remains the original recording.

Settings

Used for Whisper transcription and LLM summarization. Stored only in this browser (localStorage).

Near-live draft is optional for long meetings. When Off, Splice records fully then runs one high-accuracy batch transcription. When enabled, progressive drafts appear at the chosen interval; the final batch still runs when you stop and becomes the source of truth.

Meetings longer than ~15–20 minutes (or still over ~20 MB after optimize) are split automatically so Groq never returns “Request Entity Too Large.” Each piece is transcribed, then stitched into one continuous final transcript. Chunk length may be shortened slightly to stay under the upload limit.
